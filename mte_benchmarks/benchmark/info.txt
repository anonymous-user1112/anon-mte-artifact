The Cortex-A510 has a private L1I, L1D, and cluster-wide L2 cache.
L1 I-Cache / D-Cache	32KB or 64KB
L2 Cache	Optional, 128KB, 192KB, 256KB, 384KB, 512KB
L3 Cache	Optional, 256KB to 16MB

Cortex A715
L1 I-Cache and D-Cache	32KB or 64KB
L2 Cache	128KB, 256KB or 512KB
L3 Cache	Optional, 256KB to 16MB

Cortex X3   
L1 cache	128 KiB (64 KiB I-cache with parity, 64 KiB D-cache) per core
L2 cache	512–1024 KiB per core
L3 cache	512 KiB – 16 MiB (optional)
